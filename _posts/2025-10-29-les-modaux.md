date: 2025-10-29
category: grammaire
---

Les modaux expriment capacité, obligation, possibilité :

- *can* → capacité : *I can swim.*
- *must* → obligation : *You must stop.*
- *should* → conseil : *You should sleep.*
- *might* → possibilité : *It might rain.*
